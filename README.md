# #75 The Word

A minimalist daily reflection tool that challenges you to define your entire day with exactly one word. It’s not a diary; it’s a distillation of your core experience.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Type your word, set it, and face the truth of your day.
- **Micro-Journaling**: Eliminates the burden of writing long entries, focusing instead on high-level abstraction.
- **The Power of One**: By forcing a choice of a single word, you exercise your "essentialist" muscle to identify what truly mattered today.

## Features
- **Strict One-Word Entry**: Prevents multi-word sentences to ensure the purity of the reflection.
- **Dynamic Typography**: Your chosen word is displayed in massive, bold type to command your attention.
- **Visual Fading (History)**: Past 7 days of words are listed below, gradually fading into white as they recede into the past.
- **24-Hour Cycle**: Automatically resets at 0:00 (local time) to provide a fresh canvas every morning.
- **Quick Correction**: Tap the large displayed word to re-edit if you change your mind before the day ends.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Color**: #333
- **Typography**: Heavy sans-serif with dynamic font-sizing to handle words of any length.
- **Border Radius**: 15px

## Privacy & Performance
- **No Login**: Start your daily ritual instantly.
- **No Tracking**: Your words are stored strictly in your browser's LocalStorage. Private and local.
- **No Ads**: No distractions during your moment of reflection.
- **Browser Only**: Optimized for mobile and low-spec devices with zero external dependencies.
